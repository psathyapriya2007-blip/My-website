Political journey:

From Cinema to Politics

Vijay, one of the biggest stars in Tamil cinema, gradually showed interest in social and political issues through his film speeches and public appearances. Over the past few years, his messages focused on education, corruption, youth empowerment, and governance — which laid the foundation for his political entry.

Tamilaga vetri kalagam(TVK)

In 2024, Vijay officially launched his political party called Tamilaga Vettri Kazhagam (TVK), which means “Tamil Nadu Victory Federation.”

Core focus areas:
